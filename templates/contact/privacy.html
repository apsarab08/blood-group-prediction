Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Blood Group Prediction project ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use our website and services, including when you upload fingerprint images for blood-group prediction.

1. Data We Collect

  • Fingerprint images (JPG/PNG) uploaded by users for analysis.
  • Contact information (name, email, role) provided via the contact form.
  • Usage data such as timestamps, IP addresses, analytics.

2. Purpose of Processing

We process the data to:

  • Provide the prediction service.
  • Improve machine learning models and research outcomes.
  • Respond to inquiries via the contact form.

3. Storage & Retention

Uploaded images and contact messages are stored securely. Images may be retained for up to 1 year for research unless you request deletion earlier. Contact messages may be stored for 3 years.

4. Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. Anonymized data may be used for research publications. Data is shared with service providers only when necessary for system operation.

5. Security

We apply reasonable safeguards including secure storage and HTTPS when deployed. However, no system is 100% secure—avoid uploading highly sensitive information.

6. Your Rights

  • Access — Request copies of your data.
  • Correction — Request updates to inaccurate information.
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your images or messages.

7. Research & Publication

We prefer anonymized and aggregated datasets for research. No publication will reveal individual identities.

8. Contact

For privacy requests email: 1bi22cs063@bit-bangalore.edu.in

This policy applies to the Blood Group Prediction prototype. If deployed publicly, please update the policy to match hosting and storage details.
